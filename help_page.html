Help Page

Objectives

Your objective is to pass levels by shooting enemies.
Make sure to avoid hostile bullets!

Enemies

The health, speed, and firing rate of each enemy is determined by their shape and colour.
Note that these power ups compound over shape and colour.
i.e. a yellow triangle will be extra fast and have a fast firing rate because of its shape and colour.
i.e. a red hexagon will have extra health and less speed because of its shape, and will explode on death because of its colour.
